#findthemasks

This site is part of the getusppe.org coalition.

America’s frontline healthcare workers are treating COVID-19 patients, in some cases without adequate protective gear. Some have taken to Twitter on #GetMePPE to ask for help.

We need to help the helpers, right now.

We need to find the masks.

Look in your basement, your emergency kit, your bug out bag, your workbench for N95 masks. If you find surgical masks, those are also in short supply, and in many places are being used in place of N95s. All of these masks can save lives now if you get them into the hands of health care workers.

Other types of PPE like disposable booties, safety goggles, and disposable suits are also in short supply. If you have these things or know someone who might (HVAC installers? Construction workers?) please share this site with them. Large donors can register at getusppe.org/give.

Here's what you can do:

  1. Look at our list of sites below. You can filter by state or supplies needed.
  2. If there's a site near you, drop off following their instructions.
  3. If you are donating open packages, please put them in a ziploc bag with a note saying whether they have been used, whether anyone in your household has been sick or travelled outside the country, and the expiration date if known.
  4. If there's not a site near you, visit getusppe.org to let them know what you have available.
